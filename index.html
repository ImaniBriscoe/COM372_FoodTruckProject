Located in Philadelphia, Briscoe's Bean Truck is the only mobile purveyor of artisanal refried beans & savory fillings.

Launched in 2012 and now appearing at street fairs, business parks, The Italian Market, catering events & introducing the best refried bean food truck in the country - NOW on a street near you.

We make each refried bean meal in front of you, made to order and we make them the old school way using cast iron, flame, fresh ingredients and homemade batter.

Named one of the ten most awesome Asian-fusion food trucks in America.The bean truck serves affordable and tasty meals based on that humble but delicious ingredient: legumes.
